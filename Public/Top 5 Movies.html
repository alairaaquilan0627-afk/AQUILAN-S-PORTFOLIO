The Best Movies of all time

My Top 5 Movies.

The Penthouse: War in Life

The Penthouse is one of the best drama beacause of its intense plot, complex characters and unexpected twists, which engage viewers emotionally.

The Willoughbys

The Willoughbys is the best cartoon movie because of its compelling storyline and have a lot of lessons can be learned.

The Uncanny Counter

The Uncanny Counter is the best action kdrama because of its story of a six people willing to help others.

True Beauty

True Beauty is one of the best Rom-coms for me because you will learn that you don't have to be beautiful to be loved by someone.

It's Okay Not To Be Okay

It's Okay Not To Be Okay is the best kdrama because you don't have to be normal to be loved by others, and there is always someone that will help you when you're not okay.
