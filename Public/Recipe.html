ADOBONG PUSIT

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of medium-sized squid
  • 1 piece of onion
  • 2 pieces of tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of vinegar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 5 cloves of crushed garlic
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoon of cooking oil

Procedures

  1. Heat a cooking pot then pour in soy sauce, vinegar, and water then bring to a boil.
  2. Add the squid and wait for the liquid to re-boil.
  3. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Turn off the heat then separate the squid from the liquid. Set aside.
  5. Pour in cooking oil on a separate cooking pot then apply heat.
  6. When the oil is hot enough, put the garlic, onion, and tomatoes.
  7. Put the squid then cook for a few seconds.
  8. Pour in the soy sauce-vinegar-water mixture that was used to cook the squid a while back.
  9. Bring to a boik and simmer for 3 minutes.
  10. Add the salt, pepper and sugar then stir. Simmer for 3 minutes.
  11. Transfer to a serving bowl then serve.

Equipment

  • Cooking Pot
  • Ladle
  • Knife
  • Chopping Board
